_name

The name attribute indicates the name of the contact that will be sent to the originating email of the site.

_email

The email attribute indicates the email of the contact that will be sent to the site's originating email.

_message

The message attribute indicates the message of the contact that will be sent to the originating email of the site.

_subject

The subject attribute is indicates the title of the email that will be sent to the originating email of the site. If only a standard value will be given.

_url_success

The url_success attribute indicates the page that will be redirected to the request after sending the email, and if not passed the parameter will be redirected to our default page.
